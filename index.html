4. Interview Communication Strategy

Solving the problem is 50% of the job. Communicating it is the other 50%.

  1. 1
    Clarify: Ask questions about constraints and edge cases. Show the interviewer you're thinking before coding.
  2. 2
    Brute-Force: Always state the simple, obvious solution first. Analyze its complexity.
  3. 3
    Optimize: Explain the bottleneck and your plan to optimize. Justify your approach with complexity analysis.
  4. 4
    Code: Write clean, readable code. Talk while you code, explaining your logic.
  5. 5
    Test: Dry run with examples and discuss edge cases you would test against.
